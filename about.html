No straight lines

Innovation, like exploration, has many spirals and zig-zags. Twists and turns.

To co-founding a successful creative agency in London. Then spectacularly failing at running a software development house in Kenya. Heavily in debt, broke and close to rock bottom. And rising again to Head of Technical Solutions to help a London fintech company grow globally across 5 continents. I have gone from developing cutting edge technology for the biggest investment banks on the planet. To co-founding a successful creative agency in London.
