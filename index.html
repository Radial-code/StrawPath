Hi, I'm Andrew Mugoya

Founder and Principal at Strawpath.

My journey consists of 25+ years of innovation, solution design and product development experience. Prior to Strawpath, I was Head of Technical Solutions at Smart Pension, a global Fintech. Previously, I founded a creative agency in London and a software development house in Kenya. I started my career working in technology at JP Morgan, Goldman Sachs and Barclays in London. I studied Computer Science at Oxford University (MSc) and Warwick University (BEng, First Class Honours). I have also taught as a part-time lecturer at Strathmore University in Kenya.
